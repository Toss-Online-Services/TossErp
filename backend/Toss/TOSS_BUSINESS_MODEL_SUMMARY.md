# TOSS Business Model Summary

## Mission
Empower South African township and rural SMMEs (spaza shops, chisa nyamas) with modern ERP-III platform enhanced by AI Copilot and collaborative network.

## Core Problems Addressed
1. **Poor Cash Flow** - No financial records, cash-only operations
2. **No Stock Management** - Ad-hoc inventory, frequent stockouts
3. **Limited Credit Access** - No financial history or collateral
4. **High Supply Costs** - No bulk buying power, paying premium prices
5. **No Delivery Infrastructure** - Suppliers won't deliver to townships
6. **Skills Gaps** - Limited business training and tech adoption
7. **Trust Issues** - Can't delegate, one-person operations
8. **Financial Exclusion** - Distrust of banks, prefer cash

## Three-Pillar Solution

### 1. ERP-III Platform
- **Point of Sale & Inventory** - Automated stock tracking, low stock alerts
- **Purchasing & Supplier Integration** - Direct ordering from suppliers
- **Financial Management** - Auto financial statements, cash flow tracking
- **Mobile-First** - Works offline, low-end Android, local languages
- **Modular & Customizable** - Enable only what's needed

### 2. AI Copilot (Service-as-Software)
- **Intelligent Automation** - Auto-generates restock orders, analyzes trends
- **Decision Support** - Price recommendations, cash flow alerts
- **Natural Language** - Chat in English or vernacular
- **Autonomous Services** - Acts as AI bookkeeper, prepares reports
- **Continuous Learning** - Gets smarter with more data

### 3. Collaborative Network
- **Group Purchasing** - Pool orders for bulk discounts
- **Last-Mile Delivery** - Coordinate local drivers for township delivery
- **Credit Access** - Sales data enables lending partnerships
- **Community Features** - Forums, peer mentorship, NGO connections
- **Multi-Stakeholder** - Shop-supplier-driver-financier ecosystem

## Target Users
- **Spaza Shops** - Township convenience stores (primary)
- **Chisa Nyamas** - Local barbecue stands and food vendors
- **Rural Retailers** - Village general dealers and co-ops
- **Other SMMEs** - Hair salons, mechanics, tuck shops

## Revenue Model
1. **Subscription Fees** - Freemium model (free basic, premium R200-500/mo)
2. **Transaction Commissions** - 3-5% on supplier orders
3. **Financial Services** - Referral fees from loan/insurance partners
4. **Data Insights** - Aggregated analytics to FMCG companies
5. **Add-on Modules** - Payroll, eCommerce, specialized features

## Competitive Advantages
- **Network Effects** - More shops attract suppliers, vice versa
- **Data & AI Moat** - Unique informal retail dataset
- **Localized Trust** - Built for townships, in local languages
- **Multi-Sided Integration** - Ecosystem of partnerships
- **Continuous Innovation** - ERP-III + AI keeps ahead

## Key Metrics to Track
- Number of active shops on platform
- Monthly transaction volume
- Average order value and frequency
- Supplier partnerships
- Delivery completion rates
- Credit access enabled (R value)
- User retention and growth rate

## Technology Stack Context
- Cloud-based SaaS platform
- Mobile-first (Android priority)
- Offline-capable with sync
- Multi-language support
- AI/ML for recommendations
- Integration-ready architecture

## Current Implementation: TOSS ERP III
**Backend:** .NET 8 + EF Core + PostgreSQL + Clean Architecture
**Frontend:** Nuxt 3 + Vue 3 + Tailwind CSS
**Infrastructure:** .NET Aspire orchestration
**AI:** Ready for AI Copilot integration
